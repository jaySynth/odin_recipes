Freshly Brewed Tea

A cup of tea for your wasteland adventure

Have you ever wondered how do obtain a freshly brewed tea out on your Wasteland adventure? Well wonder no more! This is the perfect recipe for radiation-free tea.

Ingredients

  • Boiling water
  • Loose tea or tea bag

Directions

  1. Whether you use loose tear or tea bags, the prepartion method is the same. Start with a spotlessly clean teapot made of glass, china or earthenware. Add rapidly boiling water, and allow to stand a few minutes to "hot the pot." Pour out just before brewing the tea.
  2. Heat cold water to a full roiling boil. (use water from the cold-water tap; water from the hot-water tap may contain mineral deposits from water pipes that can affect the flavor of the tea.)
  3. Add tea to the warm pot, allowing 1 teaspoon of loose tea or 1 tea bag for each cup of tea. Pour boiling water over tea (3/4 cup for each cup of tea); let stand 3 to 5 minutes to bring out the full flavor. Stir tea once to ensure uniform strength. Do not judge the strength of tea by its color; you must taste it. Strain the tea, or remove tea bags. Serve with sugar and milk or lemon if desired.
